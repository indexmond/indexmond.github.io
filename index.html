Desmond Bo-Yuan Huang 黃柏源

Long Eighteenth-Century British Literature · Health Humanities · AI Humanities

英國長十八世紀文學 · 健康人文 · AI人文

Dr Huang's scholarship spans the medical cultures, atmospheric anxieties, and literary forms of long eighteenth-century Britain — from the splenetic writings of George Cheyne to the ecological verse of James Thomson and William Cowper. His current NSTC Young Scholar Fellowship project, Reading the Air (2025–2027), traces the entanglements of air science and literary imagination in Georgian Britain. He also writes on artificial intelligence, contemporary fiction, and cinema.

黃柏源的學術研究涵蓋英國長十八世紀的醫學文化、大氣焦慮與文學形式，從喬治·錢尼的脾臟論著，到湯姆森與考柏的自然詩學。其國科會年輕學者計畫《閱讀空氣》（2025–2027）探索喬治亞時代空氣科學與文學想像的交纏。他同時從事人工智慧、當代小說與電影研究。

Research
研究領域

Areas of Enquiry

研究方向

01

The Long Eighteenth Century

長十八世紀文學

Close reading and cultural-historical analysis of British literature from the Restoration to Romanticism. Key figures include Cheyne, Arbuthnot, Goldsmith, Thomson, and Cowper, examined at the intersections of medicine, the body, and literary form.

以文化歷史視角細讀英國文學，從復辟時期至浪漫主義，聚焦錢尼、阿巴斯諾特、哥德史密斯、湯姆森與考柏，探討醫學、身體與文學形式之交纏。

02

Health Humanities

健康人文

Interdisciplinary enquiry into bodily experience, atmospheric thought, and literary form. The current NSTC project Reading the Air traces how Georgian air science shaped — and was shaped by — poetic and medical discourse.

跨學科探索身體經驗、大氣思想與文學形式的交纏。現行國科會計畫《閱讀空氣》追索喬治亞時代空氣科學與詩學、醫學論述的相互形塑。

03

AI Humanities

AI人文

Critical investigation of artificial intelligence as a cultural phenomenon — in contemporary fiction (Ishiguro's Klara and the Sun), cinema, and literary pedagogy, including the ethics of algorithmic culture and AI-assisted learning.

批判性探究AI作為文化現象，研究對象涵蓋當代小說（石黑一雄《克拉拉與太陽》）、電影，以及AI輔助教學與演算法文化倫理。
